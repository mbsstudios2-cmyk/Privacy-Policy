Privacy Policy for Calc 360

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Calc 360 is developed by MBS Studios.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
Calc 360 does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.
The app does not require account creation or login.

2. INTERNET USAGE
The app connects to the internet solely to fetch live currency exchange
rates from open.er-api.com. No personal data is sent in this request.
All other features work fully offline.

3. DATA STORED ON YOUR DEVICE
The app stores the following data locally on your device only:
- Your theme preference (dark/light)
- Number format preference
- Angle mode preference
- Cached currency exchange rates

This data never leaves your device.

4. THIRD PARTY SERVICES
The app uses the Exchange Rates API (open.er-api.com) for live currency
rates. Please refer to their privacy policy for details on their data
practices.

5. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
Calc 360 does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children
under the age of 13.

6. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted
on this page with an updated date.
